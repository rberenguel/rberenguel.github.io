Strawberry seedlings: Showing leaves

September 17, 2008     1 minutes read | 106 words

Just a quick update about my tiny strawberries. A lot more have germinated, and now the first one to do so are showing their first true, small and beautiful leaves. I also added some more soil and add a few drops of water with a little fertilizer (from what I gave my other plants).
