Do you Feel the Smell of Burning Wood? Pyrography Box Beta

Dec 27, 2010     2 minutes read | 388 words
Santa brought me the gift I wanted for Christmas: a pyrograph. In case you don’t know, a pyrograph is a tool to draw over wood, by burning it. As you may already know, I love drawing (in particular, ink drawing). This is similar: you don’t have a lot of control over tone and a mistake can ruin the drawing. This was the first time I used one, and to test how it worked I decided to draw a Celtic knot over a wooden box.
