Limiting Factors: Biology Enters Time Management

What? Biology and time management? Limiting factors? Okay, take it easy. First, let’s read this Wikipedia entry: A limiting factor or limiting resource is a factor that controls a process, such as organism growth or species population, size, or distribution. The availability of food, predation pressure, or availability of shelter are examples of factors that could be limiting for an organism. An example of a limiting factor is sunlight in the rainforest, where growth is limited to all plants in the understory unless more light becomes available.
